Death To Public Schools

Funding for K-12 schools has been in decline for a while and that is even worsen by states cutting taxes-their main source of funding. Even though data from the U.S. Census Bureau shows an increase in spending in 2015, 29 states were still struggling to bring up spending per student to pre-recession funding of 2008.This under-funding amounts to nearly $150 billion annually according to the Century Foundation which robs 30 million children of resources.
